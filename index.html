BOXKID VS THE OCEAN

Boxkid vs. the Ocean is a webcomic that is currently in development by FLARE145. It will feature a character named Boxkid as he tries to do anything at all. The comic will hosted here in a 1:1 square page format, but it will likely be posted on webtoon as well in a scrolling format. This is a story that is not written from the start, meaning that unlike many of my other projects, I'm not quite sure how it ends. This is a very low risk project but I will still put plenty of efort into it, so I hope you enjoy.

I'm currently posting most of my ideas for this comic on my twitter. Scroll around and you might see something cool.
